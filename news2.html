AC MILAN V GENOA: COME DOWN TO SAN SIRO

28 March 2022

Tickets are on sale for the game against the Rossoblù on 15 April

Following the 3-0 win in the reverse fixture at Marassi with an Ibrahimović goal from a free-kick and a Messias brace, AC Milan will host Genoa on 15 April at 21:00 on Serie A matchday 33. The two sides met at San Siro in April last season too and the Rossoneri won 2-1 thanks to a Rebić goal and an own goal - Destro levelled it at 1-1 before the Rossoneri's eventual winner.
TICKETS
Tickets for AC Milan v Genoa will be available online at singletickets.acmilan.com, at the Casa Milan Ticket Office and at the stadium ticket offices (starting from 17.00). Sales windows are as follows:

  • The sales window for season ticket-holders and Cuore Rossoneri card-holders will start at 12:00 CEST on Tuesday 29 March and end at 23:59 CEST on Friday 1 April with tickets to be sold at a discounted price.
  • General sale will also start at 12:00 CEST on Tuesday 29 March and will remain open until all tickets are sold out.
Tickets prices for AC Milan v Genoa start from €5 for ticket holders and CRN card-holders and from €9 for general sale. Under-16s will be able to purchase a ticket from €5 in selected sections of the grounds - if accompanied by an adult - and discounted ticket prices for Over 60s start at €9.

FRIENDS PROMO: BRING A FRIEND TO THE STADIUM

For AC Milan v Genoa, every fan that purchases a ticket at full price may purchase a second ticket at a 50% discount. This promo, which is valid in selected sections of the ground, is limited in number and discounted tickets may run out during general sale.

ONLINE HOSPITALITY

Hospitality packages are on offer for AC Milan v Genoa and are available for purchase via the VIP Hospitality section on singletickets.acmilan.com. Prices start from €139.
