FIORENTINA AND ATALANTA: ALL TOGETHER AT SAN SIRO

31 March 2022

Serie A 2021/22 is approaching its climax and AC Milan are getting ready for their final two home games of the season against Fiorentina and Atalanta, before bidding our home crowd farewell until next season. Against the Viola on matchday 35, AC Milan will try to get revenge for the 4-3 defeat in the reverse fixture. Against the Dea on matchday 37, meanwhile, the Rossoneri will be hoping to build on the 3-2 win in Bergamo.
TICKETS Tickets for AC Milan v Atalanta are TEMPORARILY SOLD OUT Tickets for AC Milan v Fiorentina are still available online via singletickets.acmilan.com and at the Ticket Office at Casa Milan. the following sale waves are as follows::

  • AThe sales window for season ticket-holders and Cuore Rossoneri Card-holders will begin at 12:00 CEST on Friday 1 April and will last until 23:59 CEST on Monday 4 April, with tickets on offer at a discounted price.
  • General sale will also begin at 12:00 CET on Friday 1 April and will remain open until tickets are sold out.
Ticket prices for AC Milan v Fiorentina and AC Milan v Atalanta start from €5 for season ticket-holders and CRN-holders and from €9 on general sale. Under-6s can purchase a ticket in certain sections of the ground for €5 if accompanied by an adult. Discounted prices for Over 60s start at €9.
ONLINE HOSPITALITY
Hospitality packages are also available for AC Milan v Fiorentina and AC Milan v Atalanta, as always, via singletickets.acmilan.com. Hospitality packages start from €129.
The exact dates and times have not yet been announced by Lega Serie A; we would like to advise you to be aware of possible changes to kick-off times and dates before you purchase your tickets. Furthermore, as per the latest rules, a basic green pass is now sufficient to be granted access to the stadium, but the use of an FFP2 mask remains mandatory.
